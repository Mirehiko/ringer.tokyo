2018.08.16 Release
"The Man from the Sea" is the latest movie by director Koji Fukuda, a rising star in the world of film and winner of the 2016 Jury Prize in the Un Certain Regard category of the 69th Cannes Film Festival for his film, "Harmonium." TAIYOKIKAKU R&D was involved in the production of the film, and the VFX was handled by +Ring. The film is currently being shown at Kineca Omori in Tokyo, so don't miss it! You're certain to be enthralled by this moving and beautiful fantasy film!
